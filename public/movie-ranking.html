The Best Movies According To Jackson

Top 3 Movies of all time

Akira

Themes of god and power corrupting colide in a mass of flesh that grotesquely depicts mankinds flaws.

The Island

An interesting tale set in a more interesting backdrop. The set design and overall artistic direction fills me with deep nostalgia for days playing portal.

A Cabin In The Woods

A must watch for any lover of the horror genre, what appears to be a cliche by the book plot quickly turns regular tropes on their heads in a unique parody of the genre.
